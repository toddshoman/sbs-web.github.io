The Documentary

Documenting the Frontier of Reality

Science Beyond Senses (in Production 2019) is a Documentary Series that tells the developing story at the intersection of cutting-edge technologies and ancient aspects of the human condition. Can science finally explain what we have only sensed has existed in the universe? Journey along and find out!

"Imagination is more important than knowledge." - Albert Einstein

Principle photography has begun and we are excited to share with you the stories of prominent parapsychologists, clairvoyants, mediums, telepaths, psychokinetic practitioners, neuroscientists, psychologists, and many more experts in their respective fields.
